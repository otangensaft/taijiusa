About the Instructor

Master Weijun Tang is the fourth generation descendant of Beijing Chen Style Taiji. He is a member of Chinese Martial Arts Association and Beijing Martial Arts Association, having started practicing Taijiquan in his youth. With the instruction of Master Xie, He learnt Old Frame First Road and Second Road, New Frame First Road and Second Road, Taiji swords, Taiji fans, 24 forms and 47 forms, 28 balance arts and 50 balance arts.

Master Tang began teaching students in California in 2014, and has taught hundreds of students from Redlands, Highlands, San Bernardino, Riverside, Yucaipa, Calimesa. He has organized taiji performances and numerous Taiji Parties. Everyone in his classes has cited feeling warmer, happier, and filled with vitality. Many students have cited health benefits and improved quality of life.
