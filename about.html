About the Instructor

Wei Tang

Master Tang received his Tai Chi training in China under the guidance of Master Xie. He has experience with private as well as group instruction, and teaches several forms of Tai Chi, with some of the most popular forms involving Sword-Style and Fan-Style. He began teaching students in California in 2014 and has taught more than one hundred students.
